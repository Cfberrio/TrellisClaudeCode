# Hormozi Domain Overview

Read-only routing analysis of completed Hormozi Obsidian domain. Source of truth: the `00-Trellis-Core/Strategy-Models/` folder inside the team's Trellis-Brain Obsidian vault. Vault root is machine-specific — see [TEAM-SETUP-MCP.md](TEAM-SETUP-MCP.md).

Purpose: enable building task-first Claude Code skills (`/branding`, `/pricing`, `/sales`, etc.) that route to exact correct Obsidian notes.

---

## 1. Book-Folder Summaries

13 extracted book folders. Each folder = one book or playbook hub.

### `01-100M-Offers/` — book *$100M Offers*
Anchor folder. Sets template structure for everything else. 10 canonical notes covering offer architecture from foundation to enhancement levers.

Notes:
- `Hormozi-Value-Equation` — Dream Outcome × Likelihood / Time Delay × Effort. Foundation lens for every other Hormozi note.
- `Hormozi-Grand-Slam-Offer` — umbrella offer concept; four-component definition.
- `Hormozi-Starving-Crowd` — market selection (pain / money / targeting / growth).
- `Hormozi-Pricing-Power` — premium-pricing posture, commodity trap, virtuous cycle.
- `Hormozi-Offer-Creation` — Problems → Solutions → Delivery → Trim & Stack.
- `Hormozi-Scarcity` — supply-side enhancement.
- `Hormozi-Urgency` — time-side enhancement.
- `Hormozi-Bonuses` — value-stack enhancement.
- `Hormozi-Guarantees` — risk-reversal enhancement.
- `Hormozi-Naming` — wrapper / discoverability (MAGIC formula).

Domains contributed: **offer design, pricing posture, market selection**, naming, enhancement levers.

### `03-100M-Money-Models/` — book *$100M Money Models*
Sequencing of multiple offers across the customer's first 30 days. Distinct from Offers (one offer at a time).

6 notes: `Hormozi-Money-Model` (umbrella), `Hormozi-Attraction-Offers`, `Hormozi-Upsell-Offers`, `Hormozi-Downsell-Offers`, `Hormozi-Continuity-Offers`, `Hormozi-Money-Model-Assembly`.

Domains: **money model design, customer-journey offer sequencing, CFA economics**.

### `04-100M-Lost-Chapters/` — *Lost Chapters* anthology
15 notes. Anthology of cut chapters from Offers / Leads / Money Models. Includes:
- avatar selection
- promotion wrappers (premium/free/discount)
- CFA + CFA three levers (LTGP, CAC, PPD math)
- value grid (vs. stair-step LTV view)
- offer stacking (sales choreography across multiple offers)
- 5 specialized offer mechanics (free presentations, freemium, pick-your-price, free-with-alt-revenue, lifetime-upgrades / lifetime-discounts / discount-plus-fee continuities)
- lead-getting employees (hiring as customer-acquisition)
- maker vs. manager (calendar discipline)

Domains: **avatar selection, promotion architecture, CFA / LTGP math, offer stacking, hiring / management**.

### `05-Branding/` — book *Branding*
6 notes. Definition + measurement (Reach/Influence/Direction) + economics + 4-step build process + authority hierarchy + portfolio (brand bouquet).

Domains: **branding, brand economics, authority dynamics, portfolio strategy**.

### `06-Fast-Cash/` — playbook *Fast Cash*
5 notes. One mechanism: limited, premium-priced, time-boxed offer to warm audiences. Plus 90-day cadence math.

Domains: **cash generation, premium-promo mechanics, warm-list activation, quarterly cadence**.

### `07-Hooks/` — playbook *Best Hooks of All Time*
4 notes. Hook definition + two-part anatomy + 8-format palette + 70-20-10 production process + 121-hook swipe library.

Domains: **hook engineering, ad/content openers, copy-level attention mechanics**.

### `08-GOATed-Ads/` — playbook *GOATed Ads*
5 notes. Paid-ad assembly line: hook × meat × CTA. Schwartz awareness continuum. Hook-by-awareness mapping. 5 meat formats (demo, testimonial, education, story, faceless). CTA construction.

Domains: **paid-ad creative production, awareness targeting, ad CTA construction**.

### `09-Lifetime-Value/` — playbook *Lifetime Value*
3 notes. Crazy Eight (8 LTV-lifting levers), cost-reduction palette (Lever 2), price-testing operating procedure (Lever 1).

Domains: **LTV optimization, cost reduction, price-testing operating procedure**.

### `10-Lead-Nurture/` — playbook *Lead Nurture*
7 notes. 4 pillars (Availability, Speed, Personalization, Volume) + BAMFAM standalone tactic + execution culture.

Domains: **post-opt-in lead nurture, show-rate optimization, sales-team execution culture**.

### `11-Price-Raise/` — playbook *Price Raise*
4 notes. Price-Raise Play, 9 operating rules, price-test math (Price × Conv × Churn × LTV), RAISE letter (5-section: Remind / Address / Invest / Soften / Explain).

Domains: **raising prices on existing customers, customer-communication, retention through price changes**.

### `12-Marketing-Machine/` — playbook *Marketing Machine*
9 notes. UGC-sourcing system: support → social scrape → community scrape → events → bonus-unlocks → testimonial competition → 6-point script → cadence. Bottom of journey feeds top of ad list.

Domains: **UGC / testimonial production, customer-evidence pipeline, ad-creative sourcing**.

### `13-Pricing/` — playbook *Pricing*
4 notes. Profit-leverage thesis (Profitwell 512-co), value-driven pricing model (3 models taxonomy + price-test table), 13 pricing rules, 10 instant-profit pricing plays (28-day billing, processing fees, etc.).

Domains: **tactical pricing plays, pricing models taxonomy, profit-leverage math**.

### `14-Retention/` — playbook *Retention / Churn Checklist*
15 notes. Churn definition + economics + Common Factors Method + Five Horsemen (gym-era predecessor) + Value Per Second + 9-lever Churn Checklist (activation points, onboarding, activation incentives, community linking, customer curation, annual payment, cancellation saves, customer survey/ACA, customer journey milestones).

Domains: **retention / churn reduction, customer onboarding, community design, customer-journey milestones**.

### `15-Closing/` — playbook *Closing*
7 notes. Objection-handling for the live sales call. Definition (3-bucket maybe model + Power thesis + Blame Onion: Circumstances/Other-People/Self) + 28 operating rules + All-Purpose closes (7 universal) + Circumstances closes (Time + Money 4-flavor) + Other-People closes (Decision-Makers 4-step + Bad-Experiences) + Self closes (Preferences + Rushed-Decisions + Guarantee Closes) + Training System (Gym Launch case + trained-vs-untrained operator economics + drill cadence).

Domains: **objection handling, sales-call closing, sales training**.

### Not extracted
- *$100M Leads* (folder slot `02-` reserved; not created). Affects routing for: lead-magnet design, Core Four channels, lead-gen mechanics. **No canonical home in vault yet.**

## 3. Cross-Book Overlap Analysis

Where multiple books touch the same topic. Source of confusion if skill routing is sloppy.

### Pricing — three distinct angles, same word
- `01-100M-Offers/Hormozi-Pricing-Power` = **strategic posture** (why premium)
- `13-Pricing/Hormozi-Value-Driven-Pricing-Model` = **pricing models taxonomy + test table format**
- `13-Pricing/Hormozi-Pricing-Profit-Leverage` = **why pricing dominates profit math**
- `13-Pricing/Hormozi-Pricing-Rules` = **13 operating rules**
- `13-Pricing/Hormozi-Instant-Profit-Pricing-Plays` = **10 tactical plays**
- `09-Lifetime-Value/Hormozi-Price-Testing-Method` = **operating loop / cadence / formula**
- `11-Price-Raise/*` = **rolling out a price raise to existing customers**

A `/pricing` skill must distinguish posture vs. tactics vs. test loop vs. rollout. Do not collapse.

### Hooks — two distinct angles
- `07-Hooks` = **canonical** hook anatomy + library + 70-20-10 process (everywhere hooks)
- `08-GOATed-Ads/Hormozi-Hook-By-Awareness` = **paid-ad-specific** hook sourcing mapped to awareness levels

A `/hooks` skill should route to `07-Hooks` first, `08-GOATed-Ads/Hook-By-Awareness` only when context is paid ads.

### Bonuses — definition vs. application
- `01-100M-Offers/Hormozi-Bonuses` = **canonical** bonus theory
- `03-100M-Money-Models/Hormozi-Continuity-Offers` = continuity-bonus application
- `12-Marketing-Machine/Hormozi-Bonus-And-Award-Mechanism` = bonus-unlock as testimonial-trade currency

Three different uses of the same building block. `/offers` skill loads canonical; `/marketing-machine` skill loads the testimonial-trade variant.

### Activation / onboarding overlap
- `14-Retention/Hormozi-Activation-Points` (find leading indicator) and `Hormozi-Customer-Onboarding` (drive customers to it) are tightly coupled but serve different purposes (diagnose vs. execute).

### Customer journey overlap
- `14-Retention/Hormozi-Customer-Journey-Milestones` (4-milestone framework: activate / testimonial / refer / ascend) crosses domains. Touches retention, ascension (Money Models), referrals, testimonials (Marketing Machine).

### LTV math vs. LTV levers
- `04-100M-Lost-Chapters/Hormozi-CFA-Three-Levers` = **canonical math** (LTGP, CAC, PPD)
- `09-Lifetime-Value/Hormozi-Crazy-Eight` = **LTV-lifting levers** (uses the math from Lost Chapters)

### Continuity offers
- `03-100M-Money-Models/Hormozi-Continuity-Offers` = canonical
- `04-100M-Lost-Chapters/Hormozi-Continuity-Lifetime-Upgrades`, `Lifetime-Discounts`, `Discount-Plus-Fee` = specialized variants
- `14-Retention/Hormozi-Annual-Payment-Options` = annual-billing application

### Upsell mechanics
- `03-100M-Money-Models/Hormozi-Upsell-Offers` = canonical (4 mechanisms)
- `04-100M-Lost-Chapters/Hormozi-Upsell-Free-Alt-Revenue` = specialized variant
- `14-Retention/Hormozi-Cancellation-Saves` = save-with-upsell application

### Scarcity / urgency
- `01-100M-Offers/*` canonical
- `06-Fast-Cash/Hormozi-Fast-Cash-Promo-Sequence` and `08-GOATed-Ads/Hormozi-Ad-CTA` = applications

## 6. Where Skill Routing Could Go Wrong

### Risk 1 — `/pricing` becomes a junk drawer
Pricing material lives in 4 folders (01, 09, 11, 13) covering 4 distinct angles. Sloppy `/pricing` would dump everything; useful `/pricing` must accept sub-intent (posture / tactic / test / raise). Recommend router-with-subskills pattern (see manifest).

### Risk 2 — `/offers` collides with `/money-models`
Offers (01) = single offer; Money Models (03) = sequence of offers. User intent "I need a better offer" can mean either. Skill must ask or default to single-offer (01) and surface Money Models (03) as escalation.

### Risk 3 — `/hooks` overlap with `/ads`
07-Hooks is canonical hook material. 08-GOATed-Ads has its own hook section (`Hook-By-Awareness`) but explicitly defers to 07. `/hooks` should default to 07; `/ads` should load 08 as primary, 07 as supporting layer. Mixing produces redundant context.

### Risk 4 — `/sales` overlap with `/offers`, `/lead-nurture`, `/retention`
**Resolved by extraction of *Closing* into `15-Closing/`.** `/sales` now anchors to `15-Closing/` as canonical home (full folder: Definition, Rules, 4 close libraries, Training). Adjacent skills must NOT be invoked when the question is on-call objection handling:
- `/offers` builds the offer that sales closes against — different layer.
- `/lead-nurture` is pre-call (post-opt-in nurture, show-rate, BAMFAM) — different operating moment. BAMFAM is invoked from `/sales` only when re-booking is needed.
- `/retention` cancellation-saves uses an adjacent objection pattern (vent-then-validate) but is post-purchase save — different operating moment.

Cross-references: `/sales` may pull `01-100M-Offers/Hormozi-Guarantees` (risk reversal at close, used by Guarantee Closes section), `01-100M-Offers/Hormozi-Value-Equation` (value-too-low translation rule), `10-Lead-Nurture/Hormozi-BAMFAM` (re-book when decision-maker absent), and `14-Retention/Hormozi-Cancellation-Saves` (adjacent objection pattern). All as secondary, not canonical.

### Risk 5 — `/lead-gen` has no canonical home
*$100M Leads* is not extracted. `/lead-gen` would route to substitutes (paid ads, fast cash, marketing machine) but cannot answer "Core Four" or lead-magnet-design questions. Build only after Leads is extracted, or scope tightly to "what we have."

### Risk 6 — `/branding` blurs with `/avatar` and `/marketing-machine`
- 05-Branding/Brand-Building-Steps includes GMEP (avatar criteria).
- 04-100M-Lost-Chapters/Avatar-Selection is the canonical avatar method.
- 12-Marketing-Machine produces brand assets (UGC, testimonials).
Risk: `/branding` over-broadens. Recommend `/branding` = 05 only; route avatar to `/avatar` (or `/market`) and brand asset production to `/marketing-machine`.

### Risk 7 — `/retention` and `/ltv` blur
- 14-Retention is canonical for churn mechanics.
- 09-Lifetime-Value Crazy Eight Lever 3 (decrease churn) explicitly delegates to 14.
- LTV math lives in 04-Lost-Chapters/CFA-Three-Levers.
Risk: `/retention` pulls everything LTV-shaped; `/ltv` pulls everything retention-shaped. Recommend strict scope: `/retention` = 14 only with optional CFA math link; `/ltv` = 09 + CFA math from 04, with retention as supporting link only.

### Risk 8 — `/cash` and `/promotion` overlap with `/offers`
06-Fast-Cash uses offer enhancement levers (scarcity / urgency / bonuses) from 01. A `/cash` skill should not re-define those levers; it should reference. Same for 04-Lost-Chapters/Promotion-Wrappers which uses naming + offer enhancers.

### Risk 9 — Customer-journey conflation
`14-Retention/Hormozi-Customer-Journey-Milestones` (4 milestones: activate / testimonial / refer / ascend) can be routed by `/retention`, `/onboarding`, `/ltv`, or `/marketing-machine`. Recommend canonical home in `/retention` with cross-link from each.

### Risk 10 — Anthology folders look like single books
`04-100M-Lost-Chapters` is anthology — 15 notes spanning avatar / promotions / CFA / offer mechanics / hiring / management. Naive routing might treat it as one domain. It is not. Each note stands alone; route by concept, not folder.

## 7. Architectural Recommendations Before Skill Build

1. **Use task-first names**, not book-first names. `/pricing-tactics` not `/pricing-book`.
2. **Build router-with-subskills** for pricing (posture / tactics / test / raise) and offers (single / sequence). Avoid junk-drawer skills.
3. **Always include the canonical-home file as primary**, then load applications as secondary. Avoid loading applications without the canonical to prevent half-context retrieval.
4. **Each skill should have an explicit "do not use when" section** to prevent mis-routing (e.g., `/sales` should explicitly say "do not use for offer construction — see `/offers`").
5. **Defer `/lead-gen` and `/sales`** until either *$100M Leads* is extracted or a sales/closing book is added. Premature scaffolding will mis-route.
6. **`/marketing-machine` is its own domain.** UGC / testimonial / customer-evidence pipeline. Distinct from `/branding` and `/ads`.
7. **For overlapping concepts, follow the canonical-home map in Section 4.** That table is the routing source of truth.
