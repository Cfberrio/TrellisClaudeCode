# Google Ads OEV — Domain Context

## Business Context
OEV is NOT exclusively corporate/workshops. Corporate/workshops is a strong priority, but baby showers and other personal events contribute a material share of revenue.

## North Star
`deposit paid` is the north star conversion for the business going forward.

## Current Account State
- Single campaign account (one PMax campaign).
- Single asset group within that campaign.
- Main problems: asset quality / ad strength issues, intent mixing, spend waste on irrelevant searches.

## Revenue Lanes
- Corporate events and workshops (primary positioning)
- Private events of value (baby showers, celebrations) — protect, do not block
- Competitor conquesting is a valid play — do not block by default

## What Is Off-Target
- Weddings / Disney weddings / terms clearly outside category
- Convention center / OCCC / large-venue terms
- Generic cheap leads with no booking intent

## Active Pipeline Location
The active pipeline lives in **root**, not in this domain directory:
- Extraction scripts: `scripts/`
- GAQL queries: `queries/`
- Raw data: `data/raw/`
- Diagnostic output: `output/fase-2/`
- Rehab output: `output/fase-3/`
- Skills: `.claude/skills/google-ads-phase*`

This domain directory provides supplementary context only.
