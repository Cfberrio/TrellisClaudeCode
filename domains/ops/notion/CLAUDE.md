# Notion — Domain Context

## Status
Scaffold stage. No active integration yet.

## Purpose
Notion serves as a knowledge base and documentation layer for Trellis. When activated, this domain will handle:
- Structured knowledge libraries per brand
- SOPs and process documentation
- Content calendars and editorial planning
- Reference material for recurring workflows

## Principle
Notion is the knowledge layer. ClickUp is the execution layer. Do not duplicate tracking or task management across both.
