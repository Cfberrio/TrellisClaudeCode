# Content & Editing — Domain Context

## Status
Scaffold stage. No active pipeline yet.

## Purpose
Content production, editing, brief creation, script review, and creative output management across all Trellis brands.

## Workflows
When activated, this domain will handle:
- Content brief generation (idea → production-ready brief)
- Script editing and review (preserve hooks, CTAs, direct-response intent)
- Content repurposing across platforms
- Editorial calendar coordination
- Asset production tracking

## Brand Voice Summary
- **Trellis**: systems authority, AI-native operator voice, founder-led thinking
- **CTS**: aesthetic, sensory, warm, appetite-driven — never B2B agency tone
- **DR**: energetic, clear, trustworthy, conversion-aware — specifics over vagueness
- **OEV / RV**: speed, trust, logistics clarity, clean and unambiguous

## Principles
- One message per asset.
- Cold audiences need clarity fast.
- CTAs are intentional — no soft generic endings.
- Scripts preserve the real hook, message, and CTA. Do not water down.
